About Us

Need of Thee is dedicated to serving those in need within our community. We believe that no one should go hungry, and we work tirelessly to ensure that meals and essential supplies reach those who need them most.

Our volunteers and supporters come together with one purpose: to make a tangible difference in the lives of families and individuals facing hardship.
